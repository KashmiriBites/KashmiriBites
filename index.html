12 inches Kashmiri Garlic Pizza

Topped with a rich blend of Kashmiri spices, fresh garlic, and mozzarella, this pizza brings together the perfect fusion of bold flavors and creamy goodness.

Our Kashmiri Garlic Pizza features a thin, crispy crust that holds up perfectly to the generous layers of garlic, giving each bite a satisfying punch. We use a special blend of aromatic Kashmiri spices to add depth and complexity, while fresh herbs and a drizzle of olive oil enhance the flavor. Whether you love spicy or savory, this pizza is a delightful journey of taste – spicy, garlicky, and utterly irresistible!

4 Quarter Pounder Beef Burger Meal

A hearty meal with a juicy 4-quarter-pound beef patty, crispy fries, savory dips, and a refreshing 1.5L Tango.

Our 4 Quarter Pounder Beef Burger features two perfectly seasoned, thick quarter-pound beef patties stacked high with fresh lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and a special sauce, all nestled between a soft, toasted bun. Served alongside crispy, golden fries and a variety of delicious dips, this meal is the ultimate comfort food experience. Pair it with a chilled 1.5L Tango for the perfect combination of flavors that’ll leave you satisfied and craving more! The richness of the beef combined with the crispy fries and refreshing drink creates a balanced, satisfying meal perfect for any time of the day.

4 Kashmiri Chicken Paratha Meal

A flavorful meal featuring tender Kashmiri spiced chicken wrapped in soft, flaky parathas, crispy onion baji, and a refreshing 1.5L Pepsi.

Our Kashmiri Chicken Paratha is a mouthwatering fusion of tender, spiced chicken cooked in traditional Kashmiri herbs, all wrapped inside a warm, buttery paratha. Paired with crispy, golden onion baji, and a cool 1.5L Pepsi, this meal is perfect for those craving bold flavors and crispy textures. The spiced chicken adds a rich, savory kick to the soft paratha, while the onion baji offers a crunchy contrast. The Pepsi provides the perfect refreshing balance to complete this deliciously satisfying meal that’s sure to leave you craving more!

Cheesy Lasagna Bake

A comforting, cheesy delight packed with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and melted cheese, all baked to perfection.

Our Cheesy Lasagna Bake features layers of perfectly cooked pasta, a hearty and flavorful meat sauce, and a generous amount of melted cheese that creates a gooey, comforting experience in every bite. Each layer is carefully crafted to bring together the rich flavors of seasoned beef, tomatoes, and a touch of Italian herbs, then baked until golden and bubbly. Whether you're a fan of cheesy comfort food or looking for a satisfying meal, this lasagna is a true crowd-pleaser. Enjoy it hot and fresh for a taste of homemade goodness that will have you coming back for more!

Whether you prefer a robust cup of coffee or a soothing, aromatic tea, each drink is crafted to provide that comforting, familiar taste. Made with the finest quality tea leaves or freshly ground coffee beans, our beverages are brewed to perfection and served just the way you like it. Hot Chocolate Bliss

Indulge in the creamy, velvety goodness of our rich, indulgent hot chocolate.

Our Hot Chocolate Bliss is the ultimate comfort drink, made with premium cocoa and topped with a generous swirl of whipped cream. This rich, creamy beverage is the perfect treat for chocolate lovers, offering a deep, velvety taste that melts in your mouth with every sip. Whether you're warming up on a chilly day or simply craving something sweet, this hot chocolate is a blissful indulgence. Sweet, smooth, and satisfying, it’s the perfect drink to enjoy alone or paired with your favorite dessert.
